Welcome!

Locate! is a world cities location game which tests your geography skills.

  • Click the location of the indicated city on the world map as accurately as possible.
  • Your score is the distance between the clicked and actual locations of the city. The lower the score, the better!
  • Each game consists of 10 rounds and your final score is the total of all the rounds.
  • Beware‒ not clicking a location within the time limit results in a penalty of 5000 km!

Create an account to add your scores to the leaderboard and access personalised stats‒ it's quick and hassle-free!
